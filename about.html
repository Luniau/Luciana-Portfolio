Hi, I am Luciana Carvalho

Front-end developer, based in Amsterdam.

My portfolio is a representation of all that I've learned and accomplished as Front-end developer student. After many years working with accounting, and increasingly becoming demotivated to continue, it was time to look for something new.
Last year December 2021 I had the opportunity to enrol in the SheCodes workshops which I am currently completing. I became passionate about coding and all the opportunities you can have as a Front-end developer. While completing the SheCodes workshops I have developed projects with advanced HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, API and have learned to make responsive websites.
Front-end development is not only challenging but satisfying. The reward of completing a project that was coded on my own is far beyond any reward I have ever received.
I am currently open to internships, junior part time and full-time positions as front-end developer. Please see my attached portfolio of examples of projects. Feel free to contact me.
